Born and raised in **Thane, Maharashtra**, Arun Deshpande began his carrom journey in the late 1950s, rising to become one of the most decorated players in Indian carrom history.

He won his **Men's National Singles Championship in 1969** representing Maharashtra, and later the Mixed Doubles at the 1972 Senior Nationals. In 1981, he also chaired the National Veteran title — proving his mastery across decades.

As coach of the Indian national team, Arun led India to **dominate both the 1st and 2nd World Carrom Championships** — winning every available medal. He also guided India to the 2006 Carrom World Cup.

His international coaching footprint spans **Sri Lanka, Maldives, France, Germany, Switzerland, UK, Canada** and more. Modern Pierre Dubois of France won his European Championship under his guidance. He is a life member of the **Indian Carrom Federation**.
