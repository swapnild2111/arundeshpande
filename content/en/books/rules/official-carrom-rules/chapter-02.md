## Match Structure

<div class="definition-grid">
  <div class="definition-card">
    <span class="term">Board</span>
    <p class="def">From the commencement of a board, after Umpire calls <em>Play</em>, till its completion on pocketing of the last C/m of any player.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="definition-card">
    <span class="term">Break</span>
    <p class="def">The first stroke of a board.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="definition-card">
    <span class="term">Finish</span>
    <p class="def">The completion of the board.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="definition-card">
    <span class="term">Player</span>
    <p class="def">A Carrom player.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="definition-card">
    <span class="term">Turn</span>
    <p class="def">The right to strike.</p>
  </div>
</div>

## The Queen, Coins, and Placing

<div class="definition-grid">
  <div class="definition-card">
    <span class="term">Queen</span>
    <p class="def">The red C/m.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="definition-card">
    <span class="term">Placing</span>
    <p class="def">Keeping a penalty and/or due C/m in a flat position on the playing surface, within the outer circle only, by the player permitted to do so. The Queen and any jumped c/m are always placed by the umpire in the Centre Circle.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="definition-card">
    <span class="term">Pocketing</span>
    <p class="def">Putting a C/m or Queen into a pocket by a proper or improper stroke.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="definition-card">
    <span class="term">Covering</span>
    <p class="def">Pocketing one of his own C/m by a player in the same or immediately subsequent stroke after the Queen is pocketed.</p>
  </div>
</div>

## Dues, Penalties and Faults

<div class="definition-grid">
  <div class="definition-card">
    <span class="term">Due</span>
    <p class="def">Pocketing the Striker with or without C/m and/or Queen, and/or any outstanding c/m against the player as the case may be.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="definition-card">
    <span class="term">Penalty</span>
    <p class="def">Punishment for infringement or violation of the Laws.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="definition-card">
    <span class="term">Push</span>
    <p class="def">A jerk or sudden motion of the elbow of the playing hand instead of striking the Striker with the finger. Not permitted.</p>
  </div>
</div>

## Strokes and Shots

<div class="definition-grid">
  <div class="definition-card">
    <span class="term">Stroke</span>
    <p class="def">Hitting the C/m by the Striker, directly or indirectly.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="definition-card">
    <span class="term">Shot</span>
    <p class="def">A pair or cannon.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="definition-card">
    <span class="term">Pair</span>
    <p class="def">Two C/m, or one C/m and Queen, facing the general direction of the pocket with a space in between them.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="definition-card">
    <span class="term">Cannon</span>
    <p class="def">Two C/m, or one C/m and Queen, facing the general direction of the pocket <em>without</em> any space in between them.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="definition-card">
    <span class="term">Thumbing</span>
    <p class="def">Taking a stroke with the thumb.</p>
  </div>
</div>

## Officials and Opponents

<div class="definition-grid">
  <div class="definition-card">
    <span class="term">Chief Referee</span>
    <p class="def">An official appointed to administrate, supervise, control and give decisions on all matters referred to him in the course of a match.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="definition-card">
    <span class="term">Umpire</span>
    <p class="def">An official appointed to supervise and/or control a match.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="definition-card">
    <span class="term">Opponent (singles)</span>
    <p class="def">The player presently not having his turn to play.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="definition-card">
    <span class="term">Opponent (doubles)</span>
    <p class="def">The player sitting on the left and/or right side of the player presently having his turn to play.</p>
  </div>
</div>

## Anatomy and Lines

<div class="definition-grid">
  <div class="definition-card">
    <span class="term">Hand</span>
    <p class="def">The portion of the playing hand from the fingers up to the wrist.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="definition-card">
    <span class="term">Finger</span>
    <p class="def">The portion of the nail side of a finger up to the second joint.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="definition-card">
    <span class="term">Imaginary Lines</span>
    <p class="def">The lines drawn in extension of the arrows between the Base Circles.</p>
  </div>
</div>

## The Slam

<div class="definition-grid">
  <div class="definition-card">
    <span class="term">White Slam</span>
    <p class="def">Pocketing all nine white C/m and the Queen as per the Laws in the first turn of play. Also called <em>Break to Finish</em>.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="definition-card">
    <span class="term">Black Slam</span>
    <p class="def">Pocketing all the remaining black C/m, with or without the Queen, as per the Laws in the first turn of play.</p>
  </div>
</div>

  <div class="callout-title">Reading note</div>
  <p>Words implying singular shall include plural, and words implying masculine shall refer to the feminine also.</p>
</div>
