*Carrom is played in matches of three games. Each game is a sequence of boards. Each board is worth points, and the first to 25 points (or whoever leads after eight boards) wins the game. This chapter is the scorekeeping reference.*

## Point Values

| Coin | Points |
|---|---|
| Each C/m | **1 point** |
| Queen | **3 points** *(up to and including 21 points only)* |

**52.** &nbsp;&nbsp;a) The player who completes pocketing all his C/m first **wins the board**.

**53.** &nbsp;&nbsp;a) The number of C/m of the opponent on the C/B shall be the points gained by that player in that board.

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;b) The player is entitled to be credited with the value of the Queen, **only if he wins the board**.

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;c) The player who loses the board is not credited with the value of the Queen, even if he has pocketed and covered the Queen.

<div class="callout">
  <div class="callout-title">Queen threshold</div>
  <p>The player loses the advantage of getting the credit of an additional 3 points for covering the Queen once he has reached a score of <strong>22 points</strong> (Rule 54).</p>
</div>

**55.** The maximum number of points that can be scored in a board is **12** only. Any Due and/or penalty C/m shall automatically be written off.

## Game Length

**56.** &nbsp;&nbsp;a) A game shall be of **25 points or eight boards**. The player who reaches 25 points first, or leads at the conclusion of the eighth board, wins the game.

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;b) Up to and including the pre-quarter finals, each game is decided on the basis of **eight boards**. If the score is equal at the end of the eighth board, an **extra board** is played. Before the extra board, there shall be a toss to choose break only.

**57.** All matches shall be decided by the **best of three games**.

## Change of Sides

| Format | When sides change |
|---|---|
| Singles | Opposite direction at the conclusion of each game |
| Doubles | Next right-hand side at the conclusion of each game |

**60.** &nbsp;&nbsp;a) In the **third game** for matches up to and including pre-quarter finals, sides change after the fourth board, or after any player or pair has scored **13 points** — whichever is earlier.

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;b) From **quarter-finals onwards**, sides change only after 13 points have been scored by any player or pair.

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;c) If the change of sides remains unnoticed by the Umpire or any player, it takes place as and when noticed — but after completion of that particular board.

**61.** The players shall not take more than **two minutes** to change sides.
