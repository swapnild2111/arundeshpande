*A short letter from Arun Deshpande to every reader of this book — written before the technique begins. Read this first; the rest of the book makes more sense afterwards.*

## A note from the coach

![Figure 1](/images/book/fig-01.jpg)

Dear Reader, I would like to tell you this **"MANTRA"** before you actually start reading the technique in this book.

<div class="callout">
  <div class="callout-title">The Mantra</div>
  <p>Be sincere with carrom. Treat it as your very close family member. Do not commercialize it. Do not gamble on it. Just as your quarrel with a close family member is of a very temporary nature, treat your defeat in this game as a temporary setback. Do not be angry with the carrom board or the striker because of the defeat. Remain faithful to carrom, and you will then get the expected best results.</p>
</div>

— *Arun Deshpande*
